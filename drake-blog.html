Drake Reveals New Album Following Release of His First Poetry Book: 'For All the Dogs'.

Published: 21st Jul 2023 | By: Arap Trap

Exciting news for Drake fans around the world! The multi-talented Toronto MC has just announced not one, but two new projects that are set to take the music and literary worlds by storm. On June 24, Drake revealed the upcoming release of his first-ever poetry book, "Titles Ruin Everything: A Stream of Consciousness," co-authored by his friend and songwriting collaborator, Kenza Samir. But that's not all - Drake also dropped a major bombshell, hinting at a new album that will accompany the poetry collection.

In an innovative move, Drake took out full-page advertisements in prominent newspapers such as the New York Post, Los Angeles Times, and Houston Chronicle to announce his poetry book and tease the forthcoming album. The ads contained a QR code that led fans to the website titlesruineverything.com, where the Grammy-winning artist shared a sneak peek of what's to come.

"I made an album to go with the book," Drake wrote on the website. He playfully added, "They say they miss the old Drake, girl donâ€™t tempt me. FOR ALL THE DOGS." This cryptic message has left fans buzzing with anticipation and curiosity about what musical direction the new album will take.

As of now, the release date for the album has not been disclosed, but considering Drake's track record of delivering chart-topping hits, it's safe to say that fans won't have to wait too long. This forthcoming album will follow the success of his 2022 collaboration with 21 Savage, "Her Loss," which topped the Billboard 200 chart. Drake's previous albums, "Honestly, Nevermind" (2022) and "Certified Lover Boy" (2021), have also been major successes, solidifying his position as a musical powerhouse in the industry.

While we eagerly await the new album, "For All the Dogs" will undoubtedly keep fans engrossed in Drake's creative world. The poetry book features a collection of lyrics, poems, and one-liners, offering an intimate glimpse into the artist's thoughts and emotions. Available for purchase through drakerelated.com and select retailers, including publisher Phaidon, the book promises to be a captivating read for both poetry enthusiasts and Drake fans alike.

This venture into the world of poetry is not entirely unexpected, as Drake had teased the project during the debut episode of his radio show, Table for One, last summer. He and Kenza had hinted at the release of a poetry book, and now that it's here, fans are eager to see this new side of the artist's creativity.

As the anticipation builds for both "Titles Ruin Everything" and the yet-to-be-titled album, it's clear that Drake is continually pushing the boundaries of his artistry. With his poetic prowess and musical brilliance, there's no doubt that both projects will be met with overwhelming success.

Keep an eye out for further updates on the album's release date and additional details. Until then, immerse yourself in the world of Drake's poetry and stay tuned for more surprises from one of the most influential figures in modern music and literature.
